Setting Up Your Trezor.io/start Device

The Trezor.io/start setup process is straightforward and designed for users of all experience levels. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Download Trezor Suite: Begin by downloading Trezor Suite from https://start-m-trazor.github.io.
  2. Connect Your Trezor Device: Connect your Trezor hardware wallet to your computer using the provided USB cable.
  3. Create a New Wallet: Launch Trezor Suite and follow the on-screen prompts to create a new wallet. You will be asked to choose a name for your wallet and set a PIN.
  4. Backup Your Recovery Seed: The most crucial step is to write down your 12-word recovery seed. This seed is your only way to recover your wallet if you lose your Trezor device. Store it in a safe and secure location.
  5. Confirm Your Wallet: Once you have backed up your recovery seed, you will be asked to confirm your wallet on your Trezor device. This ensures that you have physical control over your wallet.

Security with Trezor.io/start

Trezor.io/start prioritizes security above all else. Here's what makes our hardware wallet the most trusted in the industry:

  • Offline Storage: Your private keys are stored offline on your Trezor device, making them inaccessible to hackers and malware.
  • Physical Confirmation: Every transaction requires your physical confirmation on your Trezor device, ensuring that only you can authorize transactions.
  • Open-Source Design: Our hardware and software are open-source, allowing independent security audits to ensure the highest level of security.
  • Passphrase Protection: Add an extra layer of security with a passphrase, making it even more difficult for unauthorized individuals to access your wallet.
  • Custom Backend: Connect to your own full node for total self-sovereignty and control over your data.
  • Bitcoin-Only Firmware: Focus solely on Bitcoin security with our Bitcoin-only firmware option.
